Road Side Assistance

In the event of immobilization of the Covered Vehicle due to mechanical or electrical breakdown and as long as the said fault can be repaired at the place of immobilization within a maximum time period of 45 (Forty-five) minutes in citys / 60 min on Highways and 90 min on Hill stations , RODA shall proceed with on-site repair of the breakdown.

Neither supply of parts, consumables nor replacement elements, nor materials in general are included in this coverage.

In case of a Covered Vehicle being rendered immobilized due to punctured or flat tyre, RODA shall arrange for changing this tyre with the spare tyre available in the vehicle after ascertaining the condition of spare tyre and other relevant details over phone. In absence of a suitable spare tyre, RODA shall go ahead with â€˜Puncture Repair Assistance .

If the Covered Vehicle does not start due to the battery being discharged, RODA shall arrange to jumpstart / charging support the vehicle. Scope of this benefit does not include supply of parts or replacement elements, nor materials in general.

If the covered vehicle runs out of fuel, RODA shall arrange to deliver up to 5 litters of fuel (only petrol/Diesel). Cost of fuel shall be paid by customer on the spot. This service is applicable for the vehicle on road and within 10kms radius from any approved workshop distance limit in this benefit is due to practical hazards of carrying fuel over long distances.

Our Services

24X7 Road Assistance

Our representatives are available 24X7 for on spot assistance and repair at anytime anywhere.

Flat Tyre

Our technicians will replace your vehicle's flat tyre with our spare, in case you do not have enough spare tyre(s).

Towing

In case on-site repair is not possible, we will provide towing facility to the nearest authorized workshop.

Jump Start

Our technicians will assist you to jumpstart your vehicle in case of a dead or weak battery.

Lockout

Our technician will unlock your car using high end tools so that your vehicle remains as it was before repair.

Fuel Supply

We will deliver Petrol/Diesel upto 5 litres at actual cost to you anytime anywhere, if required.

On-site Minor Repair

Our mechanic will reach to you and fix the issue while you are in highways or hillstations.

Accidental Vehicle Removal

We will arrange suitable services to bring your vehicle to safety.

Quick Message Transfer

We shall inform your dealer irrespective of the drop location during a situation of vehicle breakdown.
